Project # 1: Alarm Management and Maintenance App

Project Overview

The Alarm Management and Maintenance App is designed to streamline your security management by providing a comprehensive solution for alarm systems and maintenance scheduling.
Key features include:
Schedule Updates: Users can manage and update maintenance schedules effortlessly, with clear notifications when actions are restricted due to booked slots.
Maintenance Tracking: Maintain annual and alarm maintenance records with ease. The app allows users to log and delete maintenance tasks, ensuring they stay on top of essential services.
Issue Reporting: Quickly address alarm issues with straightforward user inputs. Users can report problems directly through the app, specifying issues like false alarms or system malfunctions.
Visual Calendar: An interactive calendar displays scheduled maintenance and busy days/hours, helping users manage their time effectively.
User-Friendly Interface: Designed for simplicity, the app ensures quick navigation and a smooth user experience, making alarm management straightforward for everyone.
With this app, users can enhance their security management, ensuring that alarms and maintenance tasks are always organized and efficient, providing peace of mind.
